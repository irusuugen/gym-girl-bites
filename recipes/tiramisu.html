Tiramisu

For a Friday night craving for coffee-soaked ladyfingers and cream

This yummy and classic desert that's perfect for a quick snack, while still hitting your protein goals!

4 Slices

172 kcal | 17g P

Ingredients

  • 20g plain flour
  • 30g cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp sweetener
  • 35g protein blend powder
  • Flavouring
  • 150ml sparkling water
  • 150g fat-free Greek yogurt
  • 15g protein powder
  • 9 sponge fingers
  • 5g cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Put 20g of plain flour, 30g of cocoa powder, 1/4 tsp of baking powder, 2 tbsp of sweetener, 35g of protein blend powder, flavouring drops, 150ml sparkling water into a bowl.
  2. Mix well with a whisk.
  3. Pour into a lined 5x5 inch tin/mould.
  4. Bake at 180C for 20 mins, and set it aside to cool completely.
  5. Add 150g of fat free Greek yogurt, 15g of protein powder, and sweetener drops (optional) to a separate bowl.
  6. Mix well.
  7. Dip 9 sponge fingers in cold-brewed coffee, and layer them over the cooled brownie (don't soak for too long!)
  8. Add yogurt mixture to the top and smoothen the layer out.
  9. Optional: Set in the fridge.
  10. Dust 5g Cocoa powder
