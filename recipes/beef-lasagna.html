Beef Lasagna

Classic and simple meat lasagna to fill the stomach!

This lasagna with ground beef is meaty, cheesy, saucy, and packed with flavour in every bite. It is an easy, yet hearty family-pleasing dish beloved by many around the world.

4 Servings

523 kcal | 56g P | 41g C | 13g F

Ingredients

  • 1 diced brown onion
  • 1 diced carrot
  • 1 diced celery stick
  • 500g extra lean beef mince
  • 6 diced garlic cloves
  • 35g tomato paste
  • 400g diced/crushed tomatoes
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • Salt, black pepper, garlic powder, italian herb seasoning mix
  • 400g low fat cottage cheese
  • 40g freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 6 lasagna sheets
  • 75g low fat grated cheese
  • Dried basil (optional topping)

Steps

  1. Add 400g low fat cottage cheese, 2tsp of italian herb seasoning, 1tsp of salt, 1 tsp of garlic powder, 40g of grated parmesan into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add 1 diced onion, 1 diced carrot, and 1 diced celery to a pan on high heat.
  4. Once the mixture has some colour, add 500g extra lean beef mince and 6 diced garlic cloves.
  5. Once the mince starts to brown, add 35g of tomato paste, 400g of diced tomatoes with a tiny bit of water, 1 beef stock cube, salt and black pepper. Mix and simmer on LOW heat for 5 min.
  6. Add half of the mince on a baking tray, followed by 3 lasagna sheets, the other half of the mince, then spread half of the sauce, add 3 more lasagna sheets, and the other half of sauce.
  7. Bake at 180C for 25 min.
  8. Add 75g of low fat cheese, bake it again for 5-10min or until the cheese is golden brown.

Cooking Notes

Any lasagna sheets work, but if you're using regular lasagna sheets (not instant sheets), make sure to boil beforehand per instructions on the packet.
